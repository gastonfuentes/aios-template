Sequoia Renderings

Edited today · 9:41 AM

The macOS 26 wallpapers are designed to read calmly under floating Liquid Glass surfaces. Each composition is balanced warm-to-cool so the chrome's translucent fills always have something to pick up.

When you place a sidebar over a wallpaper, the material samples the underlying pixels at roughly 60% opacity and adds 20px of saturated blur. The 0.5 px hairline edge keeps the panel separated from the content even when the colors collide.
