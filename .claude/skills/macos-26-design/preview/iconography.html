SF Symbols → Lucide substitutes

This kit's previews use Lucide as a substitute for Apple's proprietary SF Symbols. At 1.5 – 1.8 px stroke and 16 / 18 / 20 / 24 pt sizes, the visual weight matches SF Symbols Regular. When implementing for real macOS apps, replace each Lucide name with the SF Symbol on the right.

Sizing & stroke

  • Sidebar / list rows: 16 pt @ 1.8 px stroke.
  • Toolbar & menu items: 18 pt @ 1.8 px stroke.
  • Inline next to label: 14 pt @ 1.6 px stroke.
  • Tab bar / large controls: 20 pt @ 1.8 px stroke.
  • App icon glyph (centered in squircle): 56 pt on a 92 pt tile.
  • Always set stroke-linecap="round" and stroke-linejoin="round" to match SF Symbols' Regular weight.
