Form rules

  • Labels: 110 pt column, right-aligned, secondary color, regular weight.
  • Controls: left-aligned in the second column, full-width fields stretch, popups stay snug.
  • Section headers: 11 pt uppercase caption2, secondary. Sections separated by 0.5 px hairlines, never thick rules.
  • Help text lives under its control in tertiary, max 2 lines.
  • Required vs optional: never use asterisks. Mark optional with (Optional) in the label.
  • Footer: primary action right-most, destructive uses red tint not red fill.
